Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

Welcome to Never Lose a Ball ("we", "our", "us")! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Personal Information:

  • We do not require you to provide personal information to use the basic features of the app.
  • If you choose to create an account (optional), we may collect your name, email address, and profile information.

Location Information:

  • With your permission, we may access your device's location services to help you find lost soccer balls more accurately.
  • Location data is only used while the app is active and is not stored or shared with third parties.

Usage Data:

  • We collect anonymous information about how you interact with the app, such as feature usage and app performance. This helps us improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Help you locate lost soccer balls.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send occasional app updates or important notifications (if you opt in).

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share non-personal, aggregated data for analytics or app improvement purposes. We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Your Choices

  • Location Access: You can enable or disable location permissions through your device settings at any time.
  • Account Information: You can update or delete your account by contacting our support team.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or mobile networks is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Never Lose a Ball is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated directly through the app.
